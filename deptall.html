Department of Computer & Management

DEPARTMENT PROFILE

  • The department of computer and management is one of the leading department of Mudhoji College Phaltan.
  • The department started with Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) degree program in 2006.
  • In 2007 new 2 degree programs launched by department as B.Sc. Computer Science entire and Bachelor of Business Administration BBA.
  • The Mudhoji College Phaltan department of Computer and management that aims at providing quality oriented technical education in Computer Science, Application and Management to rural youth.
  • The department continuously focuses on all round development of students.
  • Apart from teaching the faculty of department is also involved in research consultancy and development programmes.
  • The department has separate building and well equipped Laboratories with modern facilities.
  • The department has three computer Laboratories with 140 computers.
  • The computer and management department is more focus to its research base in the upcoming years and also to develop more collaborations with a reputed National and International Institute/ organizations to inculcate research amongst students and faculties.
