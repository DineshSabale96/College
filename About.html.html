About Us

Department of Computer & Management

The department of computer and management is one of the leading department of Mudhoji College Phaltan. The department started with Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) degree program in 2006. In 2007 new 2 degree programs launched by department as B.Sc. Computer Science entire and Bachelor of Business Administration BBA.The Mudhoji College Phaltan department of Computer and management that aims at providing quality oriented technical education in Computer Science, Application and Management to rural youth.The department has separate building and well equipped Laboratories with modern facilities.
