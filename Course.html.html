There Are Three Degree Courses in IT deparment.

BCA

The term BCA stands for Bachelor of Computer Applications. A three-year undergraduate degree in technology and computer applications is known as a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA).

BCS

The Bachelor of Computer Science (abbreviated BCompSc or BCS) is a bachelor's degree awarded by some universities for completion of an undergraduate program in computer science.

BBA

BBA or Bachelors of Business Administration is an undergraduate degree course.For students who wish to build a career in the management field, BBA is a good option to opt.
