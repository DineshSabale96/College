Department of Computer & Management Events

Sport Day

One Day Indoor/Outdoor game competition organized on 18th and 19th May, 2022 by Department of Computer & Management of Mudhoji College, Phaltan. This event included three activities i.e. Chess and Badminton competition.

Food Festival

To generate increased demand for local & sustainably produced food through education & promotion.To support the growth and development of students food related business. Techno Spriti

TECHNO-SPIRIT was a One Day State Level Event organized every year by Department of Computer & Management of Mudhoji College, Phaltan. This event included four activities i.e. Paper Presentation, Poster Presentation, Programming Skill and Quiz competition.


Traditional Day

Students of Computer and Management Department, Mudhoji College, Phaltan celebrated the Traditional Day to promote “Unity in diversity “which is based on the concept where the individual or social differences in physical attributes, castes, creed, cultural and religious practices, etc. are not looked upon as a conflict. Rather, these differences are looked upon as varieties that enrich the society and the nation as a whole.


Career in IT Industry


The main objective of organizing this workshop is to provide knowledge about how IT industry works. It is helpful to choose career for students. One Day workshop organized on 12th Mar, 2022 by Department of Computer & Management of Mudhoji College, Phaltan to provide knowledge various career opportunities in IT industry.


Intership-collaboration with OCC EduSoft Pvt.Ltd

“Aptitude Test- 2022” was organized on 24th March, 2022 by Department of Computer & Management of Mudhoji College, Phaltan in collaboration with OCC EduSoft Pvt. Ltd. Phaltan. This event included MCQ paper based on the C, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP languages. The main objective of organizing this event is to develop student’s technical skill and makes students ready for entry in software industry.
